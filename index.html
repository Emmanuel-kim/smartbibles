What is smartBibles

Smart Bible Reader app is a fast and beautiful offline Bible reader application containing both the Old and the New Testaments. The app has several features which include:

  • Inbuilt Notes.
  • Multiple bible versions.
  • Advanced Search.
  • Bookmarks and verse sharing.

The application has a clean user interface as well as the dark mode so as to ensure that the user gain the best user experience while using it.

What is Inbuilt notes

Notes are very crucial when reading your Bible, as they are a point of reference

With Smartbibles you can add notes easily and also include verses direct to yor notes without having to type them. You can also format your notes into the format you want as wells as sharing them with others via social media.

You can also Syncronize your notes with your email. This will enable you to recover them incase you loose or change your device.

Multiple versions

Smartbibles gives you several options for bible versions to choose from when reading your bible. this makes it easy to understand the bible as you can compare verses from different versions. These versions include:-

  • New International Version (NIV).
  • New King James Version( NKJV).
  • King James Version (KJV).
  • Revised Standard Version (RSV).
  • American Standard Version (ASV).
  • English Revised Version (EnRV).

You can make any version as your default, or can switch versions by swipping the verses to the left. .

Bookmarks, Favorite verses and Highlight verses

Add bookmarks and highlight your favourite verses when reading and be able to access them easily via the bookmark buttom.

Search for verses easily using the advanced search if you cannot remember their exact location. Type in the phrase and narrow your search history by choosing the testament(old/new) it belong or the book of the bible that contains the verse.

  • You can also highlight the key parts in the verses when reading your bible .
  • Share your favourite verses with friends via social media.
  • Select multiple verses and add them directly to your notes.
